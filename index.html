Dear You,

Congratulations! It is with great pleasure that I write to inform you of your acceptance to Harvard College for the Class of 2028. Your application stood out in one of the most competitive applicant pools we have ever experienced, and we are thrilled to offer you a place in our community.

The Admissions Committee was deeply impressed by your outstanding academic achievements, the depth of your extracurricular involvements, and the sincerity with which you conveyed your experiences and aspirations. Your passion for learning and your commitment to making a difference in the world resonated strongly with us, and we believe you will thrive at Harvard.

As a member of our incoming class, you will have access to world-class faculty, cutting-edge research opportunities, a diverse and vibrant student body, and the chance to shape your own educational journey. At Harvard, we encourage students to explore their intellectual curiosities, engage with new perspectives, and contribute meaningfully to our community.

To accept your offer of admission, please log into your applicant portal and follow the instructions provided. You will find detailed information on housing, orientation, and course registration, as well as a checklist of important dates and deadlines. We also invite you to attend our Visitas weekend, where you can meet your future classmates, explore campus life, and learn more about what it means to be a Harvard student.

If you have any questions or need further information, please do not hesitate to reach out to us. We are here to support you every step of the way.

Once again, congratulations on your acceptance to Harvard College! We cannot wait to welcome you to Cambridge this fall and see the unique contributions you will bring to our community.

Warm regards,
William R. Fitzsimmons
